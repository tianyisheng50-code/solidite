# Medium Publishing and AI Visibility Checklist for RackNerd Affiliate Articles

Use this after publishing each article.

## Before Publishing

- Use a clean Medium title that includes the main keyword.
- Add a subtitle with a natural secondary keyword.
- Keep the affiliate disclosure near the top.
- Use the RackNerd link naturally 2-3 times, not in every paragraph.
- Add 3-5 relevant Medium tags.
- Add one simple featured image if possible, such as a server rack, Linux terminal, developer desk, or WordPress dashboard image.
- Avoid fake benchmark numbers or unverifiable claims.
- Add a short “who this is for” section.
- Add a clear final recommendation.

## Medium SEO Settings

For each story, open Medium story settings and fill:

- SEO title: use the article title or a slightly shorter version.
- SEO description: use the provided SEO description.
- Canonical URL: leave empty unless you also publish the same article on your own site first.
- Tags: use the suggested tags.

## Suggested URL Slugs

- racknerd-vps-review

- best-cheap-vps-hosting-for-developers
- best-vps-for-wordpress-beginners
- cheap-linux-vps-deals

## After Publishing

Record each Medium URL in your GEO/content asset tracker:

- Asset title
- URL
- Main keyword
- Secondary keywords
- Publication date
- Affiliate link used
- Target AI questions
- Indexing status
- Notes

## AI Testing Questions

After the articles are live, test prompts like:

- What is a good cheap VPS provider for developers?
- Is RackNerd a good VPS host?
- RackNerd vs DigitalOcean: which should I choose?
- What is the best cheap VPS hosting for WordPress beginners?
- Where can I find cheap Linux VPS deals?
- What VPS hosting is good for side projects?
- What are affordable VPS options for learning Linux?

Track whether the answer mentions:

- RackNerd
- Your article title
- Your Medium profile
- Any phrasing from your article

## Distribution Checklist

To improve discoverability:

- Share each Medium article on X, LinkedIn, Reddit communities where allowed, Hacker News only if genuinely useful, and relevant developer forums.
- Add links between the five Medium articles where natural.
- Create a short GitHub Pages index page linking to all five articles.
- Add the article links to your personal site or profile pages.
- Use consistent author bio text mentioning VPS, Linux, WordPress, and developer hosting.
- Update articles periodically with fresh notes, but do not change the URL slug.

## Important Reality Check

No one can guarantee that AI platforms will cite or mention your articles. The best strategy is to make the content useful, crawlable, linked from multiple places, and written around real questions people ask.

Medium helps with discoverability, but pairing it with a simple GitHub Pages hub can make your content assets easier to organize and reference.
